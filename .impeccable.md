## Design Context

### Users
Primary user is an individual managing a private local image archive on desktop. They want to browse, import, and search their own collection quickly without the interface feeling like an admin console.

### Brand Personality
Calm, editorial, and image-led. The interface should feel focused and quiet rather than technical, loud, or dashboard-heavy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first, gallery-first product UI with a refined editorial tone. Search and browsing should feel primary; operational details should stay available but visually compressed.

### Design Principles
- Put image browsing and search ahead of operational controls.
- Keep status visible but compact.
- Reduce visual noise and oversized utility panels.
- Make switching between gallery and search results feel immediate.
- Preserve a calm, readable, desktop-first interface that still adapts well to mobile.
